Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2025

Firearms Direct Club LLC ("FDC," "we," "us," or "our") operates the FDC Stickers website at stickers.firearmsdirectclub.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Site and purchase products.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address (street, city, state, ZIP code)
  • Billing ZIP code

We do not collect or store credit card numbers, CVVs, or full card details. Payment is processed securely through Authorize.net using tokenization — your card data goes directly to Authorize.net and never touches our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Order fulfillment — To process and ship your sticker order
  • Order confirmation — To send you an email receipt with your order details and sweepstakes entry confirmation
  • Customer support — To respond to questions about your order
  • Legal compliance — To comply with sweepstakes regulations and applicable laws

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Authorize.net, a Visa solution. Your card information is tokenized client-side using Accept.js and transmitted directly to Authorize.net over encrypted channels. We only receive a transaction token — never your full card number.

Data Storage & Security

Your order information (name, address, email, items purchased) is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon. We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and follow industry-standard security practices including parameterized queries, input sanitization, and restricted API access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Authorize.net — For payment processing
  • Resend — For sending order confirmation emails
  • USPS — Your shipping address is used to deliver your order

Cookies

This Site does not use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or advertising pixels. We may use minimal browser local storage to maintain your shopping cart session.

Your Rights

You may request to view, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us at stickers@firearmsdirectclub.com. We will respond to requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our Site and sweepstakes are restricted to individuals 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
